abstract: We provide faster running times for exactly solving discounted Markov Decision
  Processes (DMDPs) in strongly polynomial time. We obtain our results by efficiently
  reducing computing optimal values and policies in DMDPs to the easier tasks of policy
  evaluation and computing approximately optimal values. We provide both a straightforward
  deterministic reduction and a more efficient randomized variant that, together with
  advances in approximately solving DMDPs, yield our results.
